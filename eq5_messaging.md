| Stage | Module | Tagline | Engine | Description |

## Core Messaging Framework

### The "Doctors Are People Too" Philosophy

This is the foundational narrative of the Equals 5 brand. It distinguishes between two physician personas:

**"White Coat" Persona** — the professional, clinical identity doctors carry at work, typically reached through endemic medical channels.

**"Blue Jeans" Persona** — the personal, consumer identity; how doctors live their lives outside the hospital, browsing social media, shopping online, and consuming content like everyone else.

**Key Insight:** HCPs spend 10–15x more time on social media than on any given professional channel. Doctors are getting younger and are adopting mobile and social platforms at accelerating rates. Equals 5 reaches physicians in their "Blue Jeans" moments with the same NPI-level precision previously only available in endemic channels.

### Key Messaging Pillars

**1. HCP Social** — The only platform that delivers physician-level targeting on social media — reaching HCPs on Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok, and 20+ channels where they actually engage.

**2. 100% NPI-Level Targeting** — Equals 5's proprietary NPI-to-Consumer data graph (Personi-5™) allows for 1-to-1 target list match and activation against major social media and programmatic channels.

**3. 100% NPI-Level Reporting** — Real-time, user-level reporting available for all channels — including "walled gardens" — in one dashboard by NPI number.

**4. All-in-One Media Buying** — Upload target lists and ad creatives, automatically activate on all channels, and receive user-level reporting data in real time.

**5. Rare Disease Expertise** — Campaign delivery across 65 rare disease brands spanning hematology, oncology, neurology, immunology, nephrology, and more — making Equals 5 one of the most experienced HCP social platforms in rare disease.

**6. Self-Service & Managed Options** — Upload NPI lists or build custom HCP audiences by specialty, profession, location, and demographic data. Full campaign management or self-service platform access.

### 20+ Channels in One Place

Social media (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, X, Reddit, Pinterest, Quora), programmatic, CTV, geofencing, Amazon, Yahoo, programmatic newsletters, and medical networks.

### Messaging Do's and Don'ts

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|------|---------|
| Use "100% deterministic" / "100% NPI-level" in promotional materials | Use "97% accuracy" in promotional or outreach materials |
| Emphasize HCP Social as the primary differentiator | Position as just another programmatic vendor |
| Lead with the "Doctors Are People Too" narrative | Lead with features without connecting to outcomes |
| Use "Verify" when describing Stage 1 of Uni-5 externally | Use "Enrich" — which may imply data broker activity |
| Emphasize precision and compliance | Overuse technical jargon without context |
| Always mention privacy compliance (CCPA, CPRA) when discussing data | Omit compliance language from data-related claims |

> **Note:** The 97% Personi-5™ deterministic match accuracy statistic is reserved for specification and technical documents only. Never use in promotional or outreach materials.
