# Equals 5 — DTC Capabilities

**Equals 5 (EQ5)** now offers full-service Direct-to-Consumer (DTC) media as a dedicated product capability — extending the same precision targeting philosophy we apply to HCPs to the consumer side of the pharma funnel. EQ5 manages audience strategy, media planning and buying, campaign execution, and aggregate reporting across all channels. Vendor infrastructure (audience data, DSPs, social platforms) is activated behind the scenes.

## Audience Data

EQ5 sources DTC audiences from claims-based data partners whose segments are activated directly into social and programmatic platforms. EQ5 never receives, stores, or processes individual patient data.

- **Claims-based patient audiences** derived from real-world medical and pharmacy claims data
- **HIPAA Safe Harbor** certified; SOC 2 Type II compliant; NAI guidelines applied
- **Segment types available**: diagnosed patient cohorts, condition look-alike models, NPI-to-DTC (patients of targeted physicians), payer-derived (coverage-qualified patients), caregiver and HCP audiences
- **Activation destinations**: Meta, YouTube, Reddit, TikTok, Pinterest, IQM, The Trade Desk, and major programmatic DSPs
- All reporting is aggregate only — no individual identity is reported at any stage

## Priority 1 — Paid Social

Social is the primary channel layer across five platforms, each serving a distinct role in the patient and caregiver journey.

### Meta (Facebook + Instagram)
The anchor platform. Highest match rate for first-party CRM onboarding and claims-based audience activation. Strongest pharma DTC infrastructure available.

- Claims-based patient and caregiver segments activated via partner data push
- Platform-native interest and behavior targeting as supplemental layer
- First-party CRM list onboarding (e.g., patient support program enrollees) via Meta CAPI for adherence and retention campaigns
- Lookalike audiences built off diagnosed seed lists for undiagnosed and symptom-aware reach
- **Note**: Meta prohibits use of its native health/condition interest categories for pharma advertisers. All pharma audiences must be brought in via first-party or partner activation

### YouTube (Google)
Strongest data-free social option. Contextual keyword targeting with zero audience data or HIPAA exposure. Strong for patient story pre-roll and caregiver research moments.

- Condition and symptom keyword contextual targeting
- Custom intent audiences based on recent Google search behavior (no external data)
- YouTube Select channel sponsorships for health and wellness content adjacency

### Reddit
Most concentrated rare disease and specialty patient community of any social platform. Audience self-selects by being in condition-relevant subreddits.

- Subreddit targeting within condition-specific and rare disease communities
- Promoted Posts native format for educational content and disease awareness
- Claims-based audience activation where platform policies permit

### TikTok
Test-and-learn channel for younger patient demographics (20s–40s) and health content creators. Claims-based audience activation supported.

- Health content interest and hashtag targeting
- Claims-based patient audience activation
- MLR review required before launch; recommended as a test-and-learn channel initially

### Pinterest
Underutilized for pharma DTC. Strong caregiver and health-seeking audience skewing women 35–55. Research-oriented feed receptive to health education content.

- Health and wellness interest targeting; caregiver behavior signals
- Keyword targeting within Pinterest search
- Promoted Pins and Video Pins adjacent to condition and caregiver content

## Priority 2 — Programmatic (IQM)

Programmatic extends reach beyond social — particularly for top-of-funnel awareness among undiagnosed patients and those consuming health content outside social platforms. IQM is EQ5's primary DSP. Claims-based segments activate into IQM using the same workflow as social platforms.

### Display
- Claims-based audience-targeted display
- Contextual targeting against condition-relevant health publisher content (no audience data required)
- DMA-level geographic targeting around major treatment centers and NCI-designated cancer programs
- Site retargeting based on aggregate pixel data (no individual identity)

### Video / OLV
- Pre-roll and out-stream video against condition content on health publisher networks
- Claims-based audience activation into video inventory
- CTV / household addressable available as add-on via programmatic partners

## The HCP + DTC Coordination Bridge

EQ5's exclusive differentiator. By running NPI-level HCP campaigns alongside DTC using our Identi-5™ identity graph, EQ5 ensures the physicians most likely to see patients responding to DTC are already primed with the brand.

**How it works:**

1. EQ5 builds a targeted NPI list of relevant HCPs via its NPI-level prescribing and treatment data
2. Data partner builds a DTC audience of all patients who have seen those physicians — HIPAA-compliant, no PII
3. EQ5 activates DTC campaign to that patient audience across Meta and IQM
4. Simultaneously, EQ5 serves HCP messaging to the same physician NPIs via programmatic and social
5. Script lift and HCP prescribing lift measured vs. control at aggregate level

No other DTC agency can coordinate both sides of the physician-patient conversation from a single strategy.

## Compliance & Privacy

- **No individual identity reported** — all measurement is aggregate at the market, segment, and channel level
- **HIPAA-compliant audience sourcing** — all patient segments sourced from de-identified, opted-in claims data; EQ5 never receives underlying data
- **State-level compliance** — audience sourcing validated against Washington My Health My Data Act, Nevada consumer health data laws, and other applicable state regulations
- **Meta constraint** — Meta's native health interest categories are not used for pharma campaigns; all audiences brought in via partner activation or first-party CRM
- **MLR support** — EQ5 provides targeting rationale documentation to support client MLR submissions
- **Data handling** — EQ5 acts as a media buyer, not a data processor; all data governance sits with credentialed data partners

All reporting is aggregate only. EQ5 does not track or report on individual consumers.
