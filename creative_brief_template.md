# Equals 5 — Pharma Creative Brief Template

Reference copy of `Equals5_Pharma_Creative_Brief.pdf`. This is the source of truth for the fields
in the hub's per-account **Creative Brief** tab (`index.html` → Accounts → any client → Creative
Brief). Field keys used in the hub's `CREATIVE_BRIEF_SECTIONS` data model are noted in parens.

## 01 / Project Overview

- **Brand / Product Name** (`brandName`) — e.g., BRANDX (generic name)
- **Therapeutic Area** (`therapeuticArea`) — e.g., Oncology, Cardiology, Rare Disease
- **Client / Agency** (`clientAgency`) — Company name and primary agency partner (if applicable)
- **Campaign / Project Name** (`campaignName`) — Working title for this effort
- **Brief Date** (`briefDate`)
- **Kickoff Date** (`kickoffDate`)
- **Creative Due Date** (`creativeDueDate`)
- **Campaign Live Date** (`campaignLiveDate`)
- **Brief Owner** (`briefOwner`) — Name, title, and email of the point of contact

## 02 / Target Audience

- **Primary Audience** (`primaryAudience`) — ☐ HCP ☐ Patient / Consumer ☐ Caregiver ☐ Payer ☐ Both HCP & Patient
- **HCP Specialty / NPI Segment** (`hcpSpecialty`) — e.g., Cardiologists, PCPs, Nurse Practitioners — include NPI list or segment details if available
- **Patient Demographics** (`patientDemo`) — Age range, gender, diagnosis stage, co-morbidities, health literacy level
- **Behavioral Profile** (`behavioralProfile`) — Where are they in the treatment journey? Prior therapies, adherence challenges, decision triggers
- **Audience Insight** (`audienceInsight`) — What is the single most important insight about this audience that should shape the creative? What do they believe, fear, or hope for?

## 03 / Brand & Product Context

- **Indication** (`indication`) — FDA-approved indication(s) this campaign supports
- **Key Efficacy Data** (`keyEfficacyData`) — Top 2–3 data points the creative should convey (include study names if relevant)
- **Safety Profile / ISI Summary** (`safetyProfile`) — Brief summary of key risks, warnings, and ISI considerations for creative guidance
- **Differentiators vs. SOC / Competitors** (`differentiators`) — What sets this brand apart? MOA, dosing convenience, safety, outcomes, patient experience
- **Brand Stage** (`brandStage`)

## 04 / Campaign Objectives & KPIs

- **Primary Campaign Goal** (`primaryGoal`) — ☐ Awareness / Reach ☐ HCP Rx Intent ☐ Patient Diagnosis / Discussion ☐ Adherence / Persistence ☐ Event / Webinar Attendance ☐ Other
- **Campaign Goals & Success Metric(s)** (`campaignGoals`) — What specific, measurable outcomes does this campaign need to achieve? Include baseline metrics if known.
- **Primary KPI** (`primaryKpi`) — e.g., CTR, Script Lift, HCP Reach, Video Completion Rate
- **Secondary KPIs** (`secondaryKpis`) — e.g., Engagement, Traffic, Awareness
- **Success Benchmark** (`successBenchmark`) — How will you measure "good"? Reference industry benchmarks or internal historical data

## 05 / Message Strategy

- **Single Minded Message** (`singleMindedMessage`) — If the audience remembers only ONE thing after seeing this creative, what should it be?
- **Supporting Messages** (`supportingMessages`) — List 2–4 secondary messages or proof points that reinforce the core message
- **Tone & Personality** (`tonePersonality`) — e.g., Empowering, Clinical & Authoritative, Warm & Human, Urgent, Hopeful. What should the creative feel like?
- **Claims / Messaging Constraints** (`claimsConstraints`) — Any pre-approved claims, required legal/ISI language, or messaging that is OFF LIMITS per MLR guidance
- **Approved Copy Platform / Tagline** (`approvedCopyPlatform`) — If a tagline or brand voice guide exists, note it here or attach

## 06 / Channels & Creative Formats

- **Digital / Programmatic** (`digitalProgrammatic`) — ☐ Display Banner ☐ Newsletter/eNL ☐ Native/Contextual ☐ Connected TV ☐ Streaming Audio ☐ DOOH ☐ Gaming ☐ Podcast
- **Social Media** (`socialMedia`) — ☐ Facebook ☐ Instagram ☐ TikTok ☐ Reddit ☐ X / Twitter ☐ LinkedIn ☐ Quora ☐ Pinterest ☐ Snapchat ☐ Yahoo ☐ Microsoft ☐ Amazon
- **HCP-Specific Channels** (`hcpChannels`) — ☐ Real Medical Network ☐ Other
- **Email / Direct** (`emailDirect`) — ☐ HCP Email
- **Other** (`otherChannel`)
- **Required Ad Sizes / Specs** (`adSizesSpecs`) — List all required dimensions, file types, video lengths (e.g., 300×250, 728×90, :15s video, :06s bumper)
- **Accessibility Requirements** (`accessibility`) — e.g., WCAG 2.1 AA, closed captions, alt text, screen reader compatibility

## 07 / Creative Direction & Assets

- **Creative Concept Direction** (`creativeConceptDirection`) — Any conceptual direction, mood, visual metaphors, or campaign territory the client wants to explore (or explicitly avoid)
- **Imagery Guidance** (`imageryGuidance`) — ☐ Real patients ☐ Actors / Models ☐ HCP imagery ☐ Abstract / Conceptual ☐ Data visualization ☐ Product / MOA animation
- **Brand Assets Available** (`brandAssetsAvailable`) — Logo files, style guide, approved imagery library, font files, color palette — list what will be provided and by when
- **Reference / Inspiration** (`referenceInspiration`) — URLs, competitor ads, or prior campaigns to inform (or contrast) the creative approach
- **Regulatory / Compliance Stage** (`regulatoryStage`) — ☐ Pre-MLR concepting only ☐ Requires full MLR review ☐ Prior MLR approval available ☐ Global / Local alignment needed
- **Pharmacovigilance (PV) Considerations** (`pvConsiderations`) — Will this run on social media? Are comment monitoring and adverse event escalation workflows needed? Note any PV obligations.

## 08 / Budget & Timeline

- **Total Creative Budget** (`totalCreativeBudget`) — Estimated range for creative development (exclude media spend)
- **Media / Activation Budget** (`mediaBudget`) — Total or estimated media investment this creative will support
- **MLR Review Timeline** (`mlrReviewTimeline`) — ☐ Standard (4–6 weeks) ☐ Expedited (2–3 weeks) ☐ Already in queue ☐ No MLR required
- **Rounds of Revisions** (`revisionRounds`) — Number of creative revision rounds included in scope
- **Key Milestones** (`keyMilestones`) — List key dates: strategy alignment, concept presentation, copy review, MLR submission, final assets delivery, go-live

## 09 / Approvals & Distribution

- **Brand Lead / Approver** (`brandLeadApprover`) — Name and title of client-side approver
- **Medical / Regulatory Contact** (`medicalRegulatoryContact`) — Name and team for MLR submission
- **Agency Partners CC'd** (`agencyPartnersCcd`) — Other agency leads who should receive this brief
- **Distribution Restrictions** (`distributionRestrictions`) — e.g., Internal only, NDA required, do not forward

## Additional Notes / Open Questions

(`additionalNotes`) — Free-form notes or open questions.
