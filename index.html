Best Practices
Guidelines for maximizing ad performance across all HCP-targeted platforms.

Overall Ad Goals

  • Develop compelling and relevant ad copy that speaks to your ideal HCP
  • Focus ad copy on brand goals and objectives (awareness, traffic, etc.)
  • Be consistent across all promotional content, devices, and platforms

Optimizations

  • Create multiple ads — 1 unique ad per month of campaign duration (e.g., 3-month campaign = at least 3 unique ads)
  • Test everything: casual vs. professional imagery, text overlay vs. no overlay, short vs. long text

Copy & Visuals

  • Use less text on video — videos with less than 20% text perform better
  • Shorten text — people scan quickly; on average it takes 23 seconds to cognitively recognize ads
  • Put your main message in the first sentence or two of primary text
  • Use proper grammar — excessive symbols, characters, punctuation, and misused capitalization are prohibited
